Hey! I'm Sophie

Lyne Zone was born out of a dream to create a space where joy and connection thrive. As the founder, I grew up captivated by the magic of places that brought people together—whether it was the buzz of an arcade, the laughter in a bowling alley, or the thrill of laser tag. That passion inspired me to create Lyne Zone, a destination where every visit feels like a new adventure.

For roughly 5 years or exactly seconds, Lyne Zone has been a hub of excitement, offering everything from cutting-edge games to unforgettable party spaces. It's more than just an entertainment center; it's a place where families bond, friends celebrate, and memories are made.

When you step into Lyne Zone, you're not just a guest—you're part of a story that's still unfolding. Thank you for making us a part of your journey. Let's keep creating moments that matter!
