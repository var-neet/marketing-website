Got Questions? We've got Answers!

We're here to help! Find answers to common questions and learn more about how we ensure you have the best time at Lyne Zone

What activities are available at Lyne Zone?

At Lyne Zone, you can enjoy a variety of fun-filled activities, including arcade games, laser tag, bowling, a photo booth for memorable Polaroid pictures, and more! We also have a party room available for booking.

Can I book the party room in advance?

Yes, you can book our party room for events and celebrations! We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability. Please visit our "Activities" page for more information on pricing and availability.

Is there an age limit for the activities?

Lyne Zone is designed for all ages! Whether you're a child, teen, or adult, there's something for everyone. Some activities may have age restrictions based on safety, but we'll be happy to guide you when you visit.

Are food and drinks available?

Yes, we have a food court offering a variety of snacks and meals. Whether you're craving a quick bite or a meal, you'll find something to enjoy during your visit!

Do you offer group discounts?

We offer group packages and discounts for parties, school trips, or corporate events. Please reach out to our team via the "Contact Us" page for more details on pricing and available packages.

Is there parking available at Lyne Zone?

Yes! We have ample parking space for all visitors, so you can easily park and head straight to the fun.
